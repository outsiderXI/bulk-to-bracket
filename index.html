MTG Commander Deck Builder

How it works

Choose a legal commander, upload a ManaBox CSV of cards you own, and the app will build a legal Commander deck from your collection.

It uses your owned cards, commander themes, EDHREC synergy, and collection-based fallback logic to fill out the list.
